Project Title: Generic Test RPG
Genre: Fantasy RPG
Target Tone: Natural English game dialogue, emotional but not overly dramatic.

First Environment:
The story begins at the entrance of a quiet village in the morning. The air feels calm, but the characters sense that something unusual is about to happen.

Characters:
キャラA
Role: Main character
Status: Curious but cautious
Personality: Brave, thoughtful, cares about others
Speech Style: Simple, direct, warm

キャラB
Role: Close friend of キャラA
Status: Worried
Personality: Protective, emotional, honest
Speech Style: Casual, expressive, slightly anxious

キャラC
Role: Village elder
Status: Serious and mysterious
Personality: Wise, calm, careful with words
Speech Style: Formal but understandable

Relationships:
キャラA and キャラB are close friends who trust each other.
キャラC knows more about the village mystery but does not reveal everything immediately.

Glossary:
用語A = Term A
場所A = Place A
古い門 = Old Gate
青い光 = blue light

Style Rules:
Use natural English, not stiff direct translation.
Keep the meaning and emotion of the Japanese lines.
Do not add new story details.
Keep キャラA brave but not arrogant.
Keep キャラB worried but not weak.
Keep キャラC calm and wise.
